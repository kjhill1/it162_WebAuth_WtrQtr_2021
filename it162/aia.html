Company Profile

KW Reality is a franchising real estate company. It is self-described as a "technology company that provides the real estate platform that agents’, buyers and sellers prefer." IN 2018 and 2019 they claimed to be the largest real estate franchise in number of agents and sales volume for 2018 and 2019.
Each independent agent is required to complete certification training and contract-completion training from an approved real estate licensing program, as well as being a state or local real estate board.. KW also offers their own training blogs, guidance on becoming an agent and general resources for success.
Website Goals

Mr. Butler has recently transitioned into the field. This website will be designed to create an online presence for optimal user experience It will showcase his professional profile, expertise, services and home listings offered, and create an online presence.
